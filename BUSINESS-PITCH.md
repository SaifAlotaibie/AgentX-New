### مُتمم (Mutammim) Solution:

**With مُتمم (Mutammim) (The Complete Experience):**

- Sends **automatic email notification**: "Your contract expires in 15 days. Renew now?"

- System **sends confirmation email** with updated contract details
- If Ahmad seemed frustrated during chat, system would escalate to human agent automatically
- Ahmad delighted, **zero support ticket created**, saved 2-day wait

**Business Impact:**
- **Ahmad:** Delighted (problem solved before it became one)
- **HRSD:** Zero support cost for this interaction
